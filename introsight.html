Business-Friendly, Fast Data Exploration

Let Users Analyze Trusted Data In Business Terms

When users work with governed business concepts instead of raw schemas, analysis becomes faster, clearer, and immediately actionable.

  • Explore curated business concepts, not technical tables
  • Built-in time, product, and geography hierarchies
  • Faster insights for reporting, planning, and operations

Governance That Preserves Trust at Scale

One Control Layer For Logic, Lineage, And Access

Centralized governance keeps the semantic layer accurate and secure as usage grows—rules are defined once and enforced everywhere.

  • Central catalog of definitions, relationships, rules, and lineage
  • Versioning and approvals for semantic changes
  • Consistent row- and column-level security across tools

Better BI: Consistent Metrics Across Every Tool

Aligned Dashboards. Trusted KPIs. No Disputes.

Shared definitions eliminate metric drift, so teams stop reconciling dashboards and start acting on a single version of the truth.

  • Identical KPIs across Tableau, Power BI, Looker, and Excel
  • No inconsistencies across teams, regions, or platforms
  • Faster dashboard delivery
  • Lower maintenance and fewer rebuilds

Better AI: Grounded, Explainable, and Reliable

AI That Understands Your Business Context

AI systems operate on approved semantics—definitions, hierarchies, and rules—producing predictable, explainable results.

  • LLMs query business-ready semantics, not raw tables
  • AI automatically adheres to approved business rules
  • Fewer hallucinations
  • Faster rollout of chatbots and agents

A Shared Understanding of Your Enterprise Data

One Source Of Meaning, Relationships, And Logic

Data spread across warehouses, lakehouses, and tools leads to inconsistent interpretations. Kyvos consolidates all business concepts—entities, relationships, hierarchies, and rules—into one unified semantic view.

  • A single semantic layer across data silos for BI, chatbots, and agents
  • Consistent business definitions applied everywhere
  • Shared hierarchies (Product → Category → Brand → SKU)
